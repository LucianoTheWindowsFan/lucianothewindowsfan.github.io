[FAKE] Discovery Kids (Latin America) Anti-Piracy Screen (2013-2016)

A 3-year-old in Mexico (now 11) didn't have Discovery Kids, so their
parents made a pirated signal of the channel, which resulted in an
anti-piracy screen. It was the first anti-piracy screen from the
channel to use Sonic CD music. They even broadcasted evil Peppa Pig
in English and the Klasky Csupo logo in G Major, which scared many
children. They even put a message in Portuguese.
