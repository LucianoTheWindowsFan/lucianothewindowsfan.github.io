[FAKE] Discovery Kids (Latin America) Anti-Piracy Screen (2005-2009)

So my 16-year-old cousin (Rodrigo) was watching Discovery Kids back when
he was only 2 years old. His parents didn't have Discovery Kids, so they
decided to make a pirated signal of the channel, which unfortunately displayed
an even earlier version of the anti-piracy from nowadays. He later cried and
even ran off his room. Luckily, his mother (Romina) recorded this screen for
curiosity sakes, so we decided to grab that tape and upload it to YouTube for
nostalgia sakes. It even has some things that has nothing to do with Discovery
Kids (such as a gameplay of Super Bomberman 3).
